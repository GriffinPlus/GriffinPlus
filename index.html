What is Griffin+?

Griffin+ is a group of people who enjoy developing software and making it available to the general public. We are primarily interested in things that make life easier for end users with modern cloud technologies, improve privacy and thus provide a broader audience with secure access to clouds.

Projects

Breezy

Breezy is a multi-platform cloud storage proxy server that aims to unify connections to various cloud storage services. It improves privacy by encrypting data on the fly. Thus Breezy ensures your data can only be read by you making public clouds a private place. Breezy is designed to run on Windows and Linux.

Status: under development
